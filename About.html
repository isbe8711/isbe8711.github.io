Frothy Cappuchino is my digital space to reflect and express surfing and its place in my life as inspiration for art, spirituality, expression, and my appreciation for this beautiful earth we live on. My name is Isaac Berger, I'm a Media Studies major at CU Boulder. I grew up behind the rolling canyons of Malibu, California where I explored the diverse coastline from rolling points to punchy beach breaks and slabby reefs, and developed my passion for surf culture and surfboard design .

This shot below is me in my natural habitat. Pure joy and freedom at my favorite place on earth, talking board design with my best friends before a fun day of surf. My handshaped narrow 9'9 squash tail single fin pig. Shot by Quentin Montoya.
